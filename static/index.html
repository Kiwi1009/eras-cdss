About ERAS CDSS

This Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) supports Enhanced Recovery After Surgery (ERAS) by providing evidence-based recommendations for common perioperative scenarios. Choose a clinical scenario, enter patient data, and run an evaluation to get structured advice with key reasons, risks, and citations.

  • PONV — Postoperative nausea & vomiting risk and prophylaxis
  • POD — Postoperative delirium assessment (Nu-DESC)
  • Chest Tube — Removal readiness and drainage criteria
